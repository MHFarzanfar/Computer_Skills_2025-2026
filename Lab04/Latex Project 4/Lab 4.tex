\subsection*{Task 5 – Label placement and referencing}
Two figures: one correct, one incorrect label placement.

\begin{figure}[h]
  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=0.4\textwidth]{windows-98.jpg}
  \caption{Correct order (caption then label)}
  \label{fig:right}
\end{figure}

\begin{figure}[h]
  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=0.4\textwidth]{windows-98.jpg}
  \label{fig:wrong}
  \caption{Wrong order (label before caption)}
\end{figure}

Now we reference them here:  
Figure~\ref{fig:right} shows the correct numbering,  
while Figure~\ref{fig:wrong} may show the wrong or repeated number.
